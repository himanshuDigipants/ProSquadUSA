Using Expert Squad

⧣  How do I ask a question? ⤋

To ask a question on Expert Squad, log in to your account and type your question in the chat box. Wait for an expert to reply and keep asking until you're happy with the solution. Our experts might ask clarifying questions to help you better..

Dit it help :

⧣  Can I edit my questions? ⤋

You cannot edit the initial question. However, you can add clarifications to it. Simply enter all the details in the Chat, click 'Send', and wait until the expert replies.

Dit it help :

⧣  Can I send photos or videos to an expert via chat? ⤋

Yes, you can add any files you think are necessary. To do so, click on the paperclip icon and wait for a pop-up window to appear. Then select and upload the files you need. Ensure your attachments are not larger than 5MB and are in one of the following formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, ZIP, RAR, PDF, DOC, XLS, PPT, DOCX, XLSX, SWF, FLV, WMV.

Dit it help :

⧣  I have accidentally provided some personal data. How can I delete it? ⤋

If you've accidentally shared personal data, please contact our Customer Support by sending an email to help@expert-squad.com.

Dit it help :

⧣  How can I rate my expert? ⤋

You can rate your expert directly in the chat. Simply click on the thumbs-up or thumbs-down icon when the question 'Do you like this expert?' pops up.

Dit it help :

Account Setup

⧣  How can I restore my password? ⤋

You can easily do it by requesting a new password. Click on the ‘Forgot password‘ button on the ‘Log in‘ page. Upon request, we will send you a new password via email. Do not forget to check your ‘Spam‘ folder. If you did not receive our email within 5 minutes, please contact our Customer Support.

Dit it help :

⧣  Your system shows the 'Invalid credentials' error. What should I do? ⤋

Simply enter your email address on the 'Log in' page and click the 'Forgot password' button. We will email you a new password, and you will be able to use our platform again.

Dit it help :

⧣  I forgot my username. What should I do? ⤋

If you’ve forgotten your username, remember it is the same as your email address used for Expert Squad. If you’re still unable to log in, please contact Customer Support.

Dit it help :

⧣  how can i delete my account ? ⤋

To delete your account, please contact our Customer Support team.

Dit it help :

⧣  how cam reset my password ⤋

You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot password' link on the 'Log in' page. You'll then receive a new password at your email address.
