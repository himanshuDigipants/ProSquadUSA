About our experts

At SquadOnCall, our specialists have at least three years of experience in their domains and are dedicated to continuous improvement in their specialties. Prior to joining our ranks, each expert undergoes a meticulous verification process to ensure we offer only the most reliable advice to our clients.

Evaluation Process

Ratings

We consistently assess the quality of responses from our new experts. This includes evaluating the promptness of solutions and overall customer satisfaction.

Mystery shopping

Our experts dedicate much of their work time to further development, using educational materials to enhance their expertise. This phase is complemented by additional assessments.

Peer-to-peer reviews

Our quality assurance team, composed of our most experienced experts, reviews expert responses. This process ensures each expert's advice meets our high standards for quality and expertise.

Quality assurance

Using secret evaluation, we gather impartial feedback to accurately measure and improve the client experience. It helps us ensure that the service our clients receive is always top-notch.
